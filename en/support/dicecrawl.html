Dicecrawl is a casual dice game with leaderboards and simple roll-and-score mechanics. This page answers common questions and tells you how to reach us.

Frequently asked questions

Is Dicecrawl free?

Yes. Dicecrawl is free to play and supported by ads. You can optionally buy Remove Ads as a one-time in-app purchase.

I bought "Remove Ads" but still see ads. What do I do?

First, make sure you're signed in to the same Apple ID used for the purchase, then open the game and use Restore Purchases in Settings. If ads still appear after restoring, email us with your purchase receipt and we'll help.

How do I restore my purchase on a new device?

Sign in with the Apple ID you bought it with, open Dicecrawl, and tap Restore Purchases in Settings. Non-consumable purchases like Remove Ads can be restored at no extra cost.

How do leaderboards work?

Leaderboards use Apple Game Center. If you're signed in to Game Center, your scores can appear on the leaderboards under your Game Center nickname. You can manage this in iOS Settings → Game Center.

How do I change my ad or tracking preferences?

You can allow or decline tracking via the prompt shown in the game, or at any time in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking. Users in the EEA/UK/Switzerland can reopen the consent options from Privacy Options / Ad Preferences in the game's Settings.

Is this real-money gambling?

No. Dicecrawl uses simulated dice and scoring for entertainment only. There is no real-money wagering and no cash payouts.
