Privacy Policy — Dicebound

Effective date: 2026-06-09

This policy applies only to the mobile game Dicebound.

Language: English | 繁體中文

This Privacy Policy explains how LingJi ("we", "us") handles information in the mobile game Dicebound (the "App"). It applies only to Dicebound.

Information we collect

We do not ask you to create an account or provide your name, email, or contact details to play. The App and its third-party partners may process:

  • Device identifier (IDFA / advertising identifier) — used for third-party advertising. This identifier is used to track you across apps and websites only if you grant permission via Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt. If you decline, ads are still shown but are non-personalized.
  • Usage data (product interaction) — e.g. levels played, taps, in-game events — used for analytics and to operate the App.
  • Purchase history — your in-app purchase (Remove Ads) status, used to deliver the feature you bought.

None of the above is linked to your real-world identity by us.

Advertising

The App uses Google AdMob to show banner, interstitial, and rewarded ads. AdMob may collect and use the device identifier and ad-interaction data to serve and measure ads. Google's data practices: policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites and support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543.

For users in the EEA/UK/Switzerland, we present a Google-certified consent message (GDPR / UMP) before requesting ads; your choice controls whether personalized ads are served.

We use Apple's SKAdNetwork for privacy-preserving ad attribution.

Analytics

We use Google Analytics for Firebase to understand aggregate, non-identifying usage and improve the game.

Apple services

  • Game Center — if you use leaderboards, Apple processes your Game Center nickname/player ID under Apple's privacy policy.
  • In-App Purchases (StoreKit) — purchases are handled by Apple; we do not receive your payment card details.

App Tracking Transparency

The App will ask "Allow Dicebound to track…". Choosing Ask App Not to Track disables use of the IDFA for cross-app tracking; the game remains fully playable. You can change this anytime in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.

Children

Dicebound is not directed to children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children. The App contains simulated dice/scoring mechanics, not real-money gambling.

Your choices and rights

  • Decline tracking via the ATT prompt or iOS Settings.
  • Manage EU ad consent via the in-app "Ad Preferences / Privacy Options".
  • Depending on your region (e.g. GDPR, CCPA), you may have rights to access or delete data held about you. We do not sell your personal information. Contact us to exercise these rights.

Data retention & security

Data processed by advertising/analytics partners is retained per their policies. We apply reasonable measures to protect information.

Changes

We may update this policy; the "Effective date" will change accordingly.
