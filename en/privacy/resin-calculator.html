Resin Calculator: Epoxy Mix (the "App") is developed by LingJi Studio ("we", "us"). We take your privacy seriously. This page explains how the App handles information — in short, it doesn't collect any.

No data collection

The App does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. Every calculation runs entirely on your device.

The App works fully offline. It does not require an account or sign-in, makes no network requests, and contains no analytics, advertising, or third-party tracking SDKs.

Settings stored on your device

Any preferences you choose (such as default density, mix ratio, or units) are saved only on your device using standard iOS storage. This information never leaves your device, and deleting the App removes it.

Children's privacy

The App does not collect information from anyone, including children.
